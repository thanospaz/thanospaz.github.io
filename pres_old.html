01 // THE STRATEGIC GAP

The EU Defence Reality

The Problem

Traditional procurement cycles take years. Modern threats evolve in weeks. Expensive, proprietary systems are too valuable to lose and too slow to adapt to new jamming frequencies or tactical shifts.

The Requirement

  • Rapid Deployment Capacity: As outlined in the EU Strategic Compass.
  • Sovereign Adaptability: Reducing dependency on foreign supply chains.
  • Frugal Warfare: Asymmetric cost exchange (using €500 drones to disable €5M assets).
02 // THE SOLUTION

The "Leatherman" Concept

One Platform. Infinite Missions.

A Leatherman tool is valuable because it adapts to the soldier's immediate need. Hawk Labs treats the drone the same way. We utilize Commercial Off-The-Shelf (COTS) hardware to create platforms that are:


1. Modular
Swap payloads in the field. From kinetic strike to signal relay to ISR.
2. Expendable
Low cost means commanders can take risks that yield high rewards.
3. Hackable
Open architecture allows for immediate frequency hopping and software patches against EW threats.
